## Trademark registration
- Required documents: brand/company name, proposed logo, a description of the
  goods/services, applicant details (business name and address), and proof of
  business registration.
- Indicative fees: basic filing from $150 per class; full-service package
  (search + filing + monitoring) from $499.
- Estimated processing time: initial filing in 2–4 weeks; full registration
  typically 6–9 months depending on jurisdiction and any objections.
- To begin: ask the customer to complete the intake form at
  https://startxdigital.com/get-in-touch — the team then sends the application
  form and confirms exact fees for their case.
